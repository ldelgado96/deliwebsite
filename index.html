    WELCOME TO BAGELS AND CREAM

    Restaurant Story

    We are a family-run, independent cafe offering the best quality bagels and lunch options, in stunning and contemporary surroundings. We offer a great place to meet, eat and socialise for breakfast or lunch, so please give us a call to pickup or deliver food to you or just pop in to say hello.

    Our Specialties

    Please feel free to look over our menus or come into the cafe for our daily specials

    Bagels

    Our bagels are kettle boiled prior to being baked. We bake our bagels and baked goods several times throughout the day to maintain taste and freshness.

    Cafe

    We proudly carry Lavazza branded coffee and espresso beans. Our team is fully trained to make all types of drinks.

    Sandwiches

    We offer breakfast all day long with veriety of lunch items. We have sandwiches, pininnis, fresh salads and many more items.
